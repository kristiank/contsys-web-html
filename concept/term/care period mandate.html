Ravivolitus (care period mandate)

Ravivolitus

Termin: ravivolitus (care period mandate ⧉)
Määratlus: tegevusvolitus, millega antakse luba volitusega kaetud raviperioodiks (healthcare mandate commissioning a mandated period of care)
Valdkond: vastutus (responsibility)
Märkus 1: A care period mandate may be an agreement between the subject of care and a healthcare provider to provide specified healthcare services in a mandated period of care.
(A care period mandate may be an agreement between the subject of care and a healthcare provider to provide specified healthcare services in a mandated period of care.)
Märkus 2: In EN 13940-1:2007 care mandate was the preferred term for this concept.
(In EN 13940-1:2007 care mandate was the preferred term for this concept.)
