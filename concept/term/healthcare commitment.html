Tervishoiuteenuse osutamise kohustus

Termin: tervishoiuteenuse osutamise kohustus (healthcare commitment ⧉)
Määratlus: tegevusvolituse aktsepteerimine tervishoiu osapoole poolt, kellele see on antud (acceptance of a healthcare mandate by the healthcare actor to whom it is assigned)
Valdkond: vastutus (responsibility)
Märkus 1: The healthcare commitment is the promise by the healthcare actor to perform healthcare activities. This also means that the healthcare provider accepts and confirms the pending healthcare mandate issued through the proposed care plan.
(The healthcare commitment is the promise by the healthcare actor to perform healthcare activities. This also means that the healthcare provider accepts and confirms the pending healthcare mandate issued through the proposed care plan.)
Märkus 2: Implicitly, a healthcare commitment results from a dialogue with the subject of care or someone on behalf of the subject of care within a healthcare needs assessment
(Implicitly, a healthcare commitment results from a dialogue with the subject of care or someone on behalf of the subject of care within a healthcare needs assessment)
