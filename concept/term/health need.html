Tervisevajadus

Termin: tervisevajadus (health need ⧉)
Määratlus: terviseoleku puudujääk võrreldes soovitud tulevase terviseolekuga (deficit in the current health state compared to aspects of a desired future health state)
Valdkond: asjaolud (matter)
Märkus 1: A health need is the deficit in a subject of care’s health state.
(A health need is the deficit in a subject of care’s health state.)
Märkus 2: The current health state is observed as observed conditions.
(The current health state is observed as observed conditions.)
Märkus 3: The desired future health state can be a health objective expressed as target conditions.
(The desired future health state can be a health objective expressed as target conditions.)
Märkus 4: The health need can be identified and formulated by the subject of care or by any other healthcare actor.
(The health need can be identified and formulated by the subject of care or by any other healthcare actor.)
Märkus 5: Health needs are the motivations/indications for healthcare activities and are the basic input to healthcare needs assessments.
(Health needs are the motivations/indications for healthcare activities and are the basic input to healthcare needs assessments.)
