Tervishoiutegevus

Termin: tervishoiutegevus (healthcare activity ⧉)
Määratlus: tegevus, mis on otseselt või kaudselt mõeldud terviseoleku parandamiseks või säilitamiseks (activity intended directly or indirectly to improve or maintain a health state)
Valdkond: tegevused (activity)
Märkus 1: Each specialization of this concept represents healthcare activities performed by a specialization of healthcare actor.
(Each specialization of this concept represents healthcare activities performed by a specialization of healthcare actor.)
Märkus 2: Different types of healthcare activity elements (e.g. healthcare investigation or healthcare treatment) may be performed during a healthcare activity.
(Different types of healthcare activity elements (e.g. healthcare investigation or healthcare treatment) may be performed during a healthcare activity.)
Märkus 3: See the concepts healthcare provider activity, self-care activity, healthcare third party activity and automated healthcare when it comes to the recording of information that are the result of healthcare activities (e.g. ratified observations).
(See the concepts healthcare provider activity, self-care activity, healthcare third party activity and automated healthcare when it comes to the recording of information that are the result of healthcare activities (e.g. ratified observations).)
