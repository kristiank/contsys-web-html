Tervishoiutegevuse perioodi osa

Termin: tervishoiutegevuse perioodi osa (healthcare activity period element ⧉)
Määratlus: tervishoiutegevuse perioodi osa, mil käsitletakse üht teatud tervisemuret (part of a healthcare activity period during which one health issue is specifically addressed)
Valdkond: aeg (time)
Märkus 1: Several healthcare activity period elements may take place during the course of a healthcare activity period.
(Several healthcare activity period elements may take place during the course of a healthcare activity period.)
Märkus 2: A healthcare activity period element is part of only one healthcare activity period and of only one episode of care.
(A healthcare activity period element is part of only one healthcare activity period and of only one episode of care.)
Märkus 3: In EN 13940-1:2007 contact element was the preferred term for this concept.
(In EN 13940-1:2007 contact element was the preferred term for this concept.)
