Sihtseisund

Termin: sihtseisund (target condition ⧉)
Määratlus: võimalik seisund, mis väljendab tervise- või tervishoiutegevuse eesmärke (potential health condition representing health objectives and/or healthcare goals)
Valdkond: asjaolud (matter)
Märkus 1: Assessment of needs for healthcare activities includes identification of health objectives and/or healthcare goals. These inform decisions about relevant activities to create or update the care plan.
