Termin: tüsistuse käsitlus (adverse event management ⧉)
Määratlus: tüsistusele reageerimiseks sooritatavad tervishoiutegevused (set of healthcare activities performed in response to an adverse event)
Valdkond: protsess (process)
Märkus 1: The purposes for adverse event management are usually two: one is to reverse the effect or minimize the consequences of the adverse event, another one is to prevent the kind of adverse event in the future.
