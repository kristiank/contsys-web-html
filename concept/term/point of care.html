Tervishoiutegevuse asukoht

Termin: tervishoiutegevuse asukoht (point of care ⧉)
Määratlus: asukoht, kus tervishoiutegevusi vahetult sooritatakse (location where direct healthcare activities are performed)
Valdkond: tegevused (activity)
Märkus 1: Location refers to the geographical location of the subject of care; not the body area of the subject of care that the treatment is applied to.
