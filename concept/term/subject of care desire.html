Patsiendi tahe

Termin: patsiendi tahe (subject of care desire ⧉)
Määratlus: patsiendi või patsiendi esindaja väljendatud tahe teatud tervishoiutegevuste sooritamiseks (desire expressed by the subject of care or the subject of care proxy regarding the performance of certain healthcare activities)
Valdkond: vastutus (responsibility)
Märkus 1: In quality management [ISO 9000:2005] requirement is defined as “needs and expectations that is stated generally implied or obligatory". The expectations reflect the subject of care's desires.
