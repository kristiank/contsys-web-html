Täheldatud seisund

Termin: täheldatud seisund (observed condition ⧉)
Määratlus: terviseseisund, mida on täheldanud tervishoiu osapool (health condition observed by a healthcare actor)
Valdkond: asjaolud (matter)
Märkus 1: Healthcare professionals and subjects of care are examples of healthcare actors that can perceive the observed aspect of a health state.
(Healthcare professionals and subjects of care are examples of healthcare actors that can perceive the observed aspect of a health state.)
Märkus 2: An observed condition is a health issue and as such is a representation of aspect(s) of the health state.
(An observed condition is a health issue and as such is a representation of aspect(s) of the health state.)
