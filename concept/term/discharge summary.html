Raviperioodi kokkuvõte

Termin: raviperioodi kokkuvõte (discharge summary ⧉)
Määratlus: raviperioodi lõpetamise teatis, mis võtab kokku volitustega kaetud raviperioodi (discharge report summarizing a mandated period of care)
Valdkond: teabehaldus (information)
Märkus 1: A discharge summary may be provided to the subject of care.
(A discharge summary may be provided to the subject of care.)
Märkus 2: One mandated period of care may be immediately followed by another; the next mandated healthcare provider will then be a main recipient of the discharge summary.
(One mandated period of care may be immediately followed by another; the next mandated healthcare provider will then be a main recipient of the discharge summary.)
Märkus 3: Discharge summaries are often meant to be processed so as to categorize hospital stays according to a statistically designed classification (such as the Diagnosis-Related Groups (DRGs) or other systems), usually for funding or epidemiological purposes.
(Discharge summaries are often meant to be processed so as to categorize hospital stays according to a statistically designed classification (such as the Diagnosis-Related Groups (DRGs) or other systems), usually for funding or epidemiological purposes.)
