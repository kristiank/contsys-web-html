Volitustega kaetud raviperiood

Termin: volitustega kaetud raviperiood (mandated period of care ⧉)
Määratlus: tervishoiutegevuste perioodide kogum, mil tervishoiuteenuse osutaja on volitatud sooritama tervishoiutegevusi teatud tervisevajaduste käsitlemiseks (set of healthcare activity periods where a healthcare provider is mandated to perform the healthcare activities required to address specific health needs)
Valdkond: aeg (time)
Märkus 1: The mandated period of care is focused upon the framework of a care commitment of the provider as well as the mandate from the subject of care, which means that the roles and responsibilities of both the interacting parts are respected.
(The mandated period of care is focused upon the framework of a care commitment of the provider as well as the mandate from the subject of care, which means that the roles and responsibilities of both the interacting parts are respected.)
Märkus 2: Whenever the healthcare provider considered in a mandated period of care is a healthcare organization, this mandated period of care encompasses all healthcare activity periods with healthcare professionals who have a role in that healthcare organization.
(Whenever the healthcare provider considered in a mandated period of care is a healthcare organization, this mandated period of care encompasses all healthcare activity periods with healthcare professionals who have a role in that healthcare organization.)
Märkus 3: A mandated period of care may be part of another mandated period of care.
(A mandated period of care may be part of another mandated period of care.)
Märkus 4: In EN 13940-1:2007 period of care was the preferred term for this concept.
(In EN 13940-1:2007 period of care was the preferred term for this concept.)
Märkus 5: The relevant information describing and produced during the healthcare activities performed as a part of a mandated period of care is recorded in one or more professional health records.
(The relevant information describing and produced during the healthcare activities performed as a part of a mandated period of care is recorded in one or more professional health records.)
