Tegevusvolitus tervishoius

Termin: tegevusvolitus tervishoius (healthcare mandate ⧉)
Määratlus: kohustusel ja teadlikul nõusolekul või seadusega antud volitusel põhinev mandaat, mis määratleb ühe tervishoiu osapoole õigused ja kohustused konkreetse patsiendi jaoks tehtavates tervishoiuprotsessides (mandate (commission) based on a commitment and either an informed consent or an authorization by law, defining the rights and obligations of one healthcare actor with regard to his involvement in healthcare processes performed for a specific subject of care)
Valdkond: vastutus (responsibility)
Märkus 1: A healthcare mandate can be explicit or implicit
(A healthcare mandate can be explicit or implicit)
Märkus 2: Relevant information related to healthcare mandates (including demands for care, informed consents, dissents, healthcare commitments, etc.), is recorded in health records where the information is made available for concerned healthcare actors as health concerns.
(Relevant information related to healthcare mandates (including demands for care, informed consents, dissents, healthcare commitments, etc.), is recorded in health records where the information is made available for concerned healthcare actors as health concerns.)
Märkus 3: Typically a healthcare mandate is assigned by one healthcare actor to another.
(Typically a healthcare mandate is assigned by one healthcare actor to another.)
Märkus 4: In EN 13940-1:2007 health mandate was the preferred term for this concept.
(In EN 13940-1:2007 health mandate was the preferred term for this concept.)
