Riskiseisund

Termin: riskiseisund (risk condition ⧉)
Määratlus: võimalik seisund, mis kujutab soovimatut tulevast terviseolekut (potential health condition representing an unintended future health state)
Valdkond: asjaolud (matter)
Märkus 1: While a risk is defined as the combination of a probability of an event and its consequences, the risk condition deals only with the consequences.
