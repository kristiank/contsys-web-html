Raviteekond (clinical pathway)

Raviteekond

Termin: raviteekond (clinical pathway ⧉)
Määratlus: tervishoiutegevuste kava, mis kajastab ravistandardite sisu (pathway for the healthcare activities informing the content of core care plans)
Valdkond: planeerimine (planning)
Märkus 1: The concept clinical pathway includes subtypes, for example, 'integrated care pathways', 'multidisciplinary pathways of care', 'collaborative care pathways'.
(The concept clinical pathway includes subtypes, for example, 'integrated care pathways', 'multidisciplinary pathways of care', 'collaborative care pathways'.)
Märkus 2: Clinical pathways are designed to support healthcare administration and healthcare resource management. They provide detailed guidance for each stage in the management of a patient (treatments, interventions, etc.).
(Clinical pathways are designed to support healthcare administration and healthcare resource management. They provide detailed guidance for each stage in the management of a patient (treatments, interventions, etc.).)
