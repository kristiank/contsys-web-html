Tervisesekkumine automaatse meditsiiniseadmega

Termin: tervisesekkumine automaatse meditsiiniseadmega (automated healthcare ⧉)
Määratlus: tervishoiuteenuse osutamine tervishoiu vastutava osapoole algatusel automaatse meditsiiniseadmega (method of delivering healthcare initiated by a responsible healthcare actor and thereafter delivered automatically by an automatic medical device)
Valdkond: tegevused (activity)
Märkus 1: Automated healthcare is not a healthcare activity in its own right since the automatic medical device doesn't have the capacity to be responsible. It is the healthcare actor who initiates and reviews the automated healthcare that is responsible for safe use of the automatic medical device.
