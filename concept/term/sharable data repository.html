Jagatav andmehoidla

Termin: jagatav andmehoidla (sharable data repository ⧉)
Määratlus: ainult elektroonilisi terviselugude väljavõtteid sisaldav andmehoidla, mis on tervishoiu osapoole järelevalve all ning kuhu pääsevad ligi nõudekohaselt volitatud tervishoiu osapooled sõltumata nende organisatsioonilisest kuuluvusest (data repository containing exclusively electronic health record extracts, accessible for duly authorized healthcare actors independent of their organizational affiliation and placed under the custody of a healthcare actor)
Valdkond: teabehaldus (information)
Märkus 1: A sharable data repository has to be placed under the custody of a healthcare actor in order to assure and maintain its consistency.
(A sharable data repository has to be placed under the custody of a healthcare actor in order to assure and maintain its consistency.)
Märkus 2: In EN 13940-1:2007 sharable data repository was defined as 'electronic health record containing exclusively sharable data, placed under the custody of a healthcare party, to whom a continuity facilitator mandate has been delivered'.
(In EN 13940-1:2007 sharable data repository was defined as 'electronic health record containing exclusively sharable data, placed under the custody of a healthcare party, to whom a continuity facilitator mandate has been delivered'.)
