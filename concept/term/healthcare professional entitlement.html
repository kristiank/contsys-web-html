Tervishoiutöötajana tegutsemise õigus

Termin: tervishoiutöötajana tegutsemise õigus (healthcare professional entitlement ⧉)
Määratlus: isikule antud registreeritud volitus, et võimaldada isikule tervishoius spetsiifilist rolli (registered authorization given to a person in order to allow the person to have or perform specific roles in healthcare)
Valdkond: osapooled (actor)
Märkus 1: Entitlement is usually backed by evidence of having received, or continuously receiving, the necessary qualification, the relevant education and training.
(Entitlement is usually backed by evidence of having received, or continuously receiving, the necessary qualification, the relevant education and training.)
Märkus 2: The official entitlement of a healthcare professional forms the foundation for his/her official duties and rights.
(The official entitlement of a healthcare professional forms the foundation for his/her official duties and rights.)
