Mõiste „lähedane isik“ (next of kin)

Lähedane isik

Termin: lähedane isik (next of kin ⧉)
Määratlus: isiku roll; keegi, kes on patsiendi lähim elusolev sugulane või kellega tal on lähedane suhe (person role being either the closest living relative of the subject of care or identified as the one he has a close relationship with)
Valdkond: osapooled (actor)
Märkus 1: The person that is the next of kin may participate implicitly or explicitly in healthcare by sometimes being a subject of care proxy when the subject of care has impaired consent competence. Thereby in these circumstances a person that is next of kin can perform the role of a healthcare third party.
(The person that is the next of kin may participate implicitly or explicitly in healthcare by sometimes being a subject of care proxy when the subject of care has impaired consent competence. Thereby in these circumstances a person that is next of kin can perform the role of a healthcare third party.)
Märkus 2: A person may play the role of next of kin to more than one subject of care
