Tervishoiutegevuse periood

Termin: tervishoiutegevuse periood (healthcare activity period ⧉)
Määratlus: ajavahemik, mille jooksul sooritatakse patsiendi heaks tervishoiutegevusi (time interval during which healthcare activities are performed for a subject of care)
Valdkond: aeg (time)
Märkus 1: In EN 13940-1:2007 contact was the preferred term for this concept.
