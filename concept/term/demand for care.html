Ravitaotlus

Termin: ravitaotlus (demand for care ⧉)
Määratlus: tervishoiu osapoole väljendatud taotlus tervishoiuteenuse osutaja tegevusteks (demand for healthcare provider activities expressed by a healthcare actor)
Valdkond: vastutus (responsibility)
Märkus 1: A demand for care may be expressed either by the subject of care or on their behalf.
(A demand for care may be expressed either by the subject of care or on their behalf.)
Märkus 2: A healthcare provider may accept or decline a demand for care.
(A healthcare provider may accept or decline a demand for care.)
Märkus 3: A demand for care could result in a healthcare assessment concluding that no other healthcare activity elements (as healthcare investigations and/or healthcare treatments) should be performed.
(A demand for care could result in a healthcare assessment concluding that no other healthcare activity elements (as healthcare investigations and/or healthcare treatments) should be performed.)
