Mõiste „terviseseisundi periood“ (health condition period)

Terviseseisundi periood

Termin: terviseseisundi periood (health condition period ⧉)
Määratlus: patsiendi tervisega seonduv periood, mil jälgitakse terviseseisundit (health related period during which a health condition has been observed)
Valdkond: aeg (time)
Märkus 1: Observation of a health condition may lead to an episode of care
(Observation of a health condition may lead to an episode of care)
Märkus 2: Health condition period refers only to the observation of the health condition, for example, the time interval during which a subject of care has observed a bleeding. The concept episode of care is referring to the healthcare activities.
(Health condition period refers only to the observation of the health condition, for example, the time interval during which a subject of care has observed a bleeding. The concept episode of care is referring to the healthcare activities.)
