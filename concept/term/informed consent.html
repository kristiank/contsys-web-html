Teadlik nõusolek

Termin: teadlik nõusolek (informed consent ⧉)
Määratlus: nõusolekuvõimega patsiendi või patsiendi esindaja vabatahtlikult antud luba sooritada tervishoiutegevusi, kui nende eesmärgist ja võimalikest tagajärgedest on teavitatud (permission to perform healthcare activities, voluntarily given by a subject of care having consent competence, or by a subject of care proxy, after having been informed about the purpose and the possible results of the healthcare activities)
Valdkond: vastutus (responsibility)
Märkus 1: A healthcare mandate requires either informed consent or authorization by law.
(A healthcare mandate requires either informed consent or authorization by law.)
