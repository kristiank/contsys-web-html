Mõiste „terviseprobleem“ (health problem)

Terviseprobleem

Termin: terviseprobleem (health problem ⧉)
Määratlus: terviseseisund, mida tervishoiu osapool käsitleb probleemina (health condition considered by a healthcare actor to be a problem)
Valdkond: asjaolud (matter)
Märkus 1: Health problems can be single observations but are usually more compound as a summary of several observations. Single observations are often criteria for the more compound health condition considered to be a health problem.
