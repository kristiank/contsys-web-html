Terviseuuring

Termin: terviseuuring (healthcare investigation ⧉)
Määratlus: tervishoiutoiming, mille eesmärk on selgitada patsiendi seisundi(te)ga seonduvat (healthcare activity element with the intention to clarify one or more health conditions of a subject of care)
Valdkond: tegevused (activity)
Märkus 1: Healthcare investigations add and improve information about aspects of a health state.
(Healthcare investigations add and improve information about aspects of a health state.)
Märkus 2: Some primary healthcare investigations can sometimes have an intended or secondary therapeutic effect, while some activities can be both investigating and therapeutic by essence (e.g. a fibroscopy, interventional cardiology, etc.)
(Some primary healthcare investigations can sometimes have an intended or secondary therapeutic effect, while some activities can be both investigating and therapeutic by essence (e.g. a fibroscopy, interventional cardiology, etc.))
